What is the 2024 Paris Olympics?

The 33rd Summer Olympics are scheduled to be held in Paris, France in 2024. It is the first Olympics to be held in Europe since the 2012 London Olympics, and the first in 10 years since the 2014 Sochi Winter Olympics if the Winter Olympics are included. It is the first Olympic Games to be held in France in 32 years since the 1992 Albertville Winter Olympics. From the point of view of Paris, the capital, it is the Summer Olympics that returned to Paris exactly 100 years after the 1924 Paris Olympics. From January 15, 2015 to January 8, 2016, applications were received, the final candidate cities were selected from April to May 2016, and a local visit was conducted from February to March 2017. Paris, France or Los Angeles, USA was decided as the host city on July 12, and the order of hosting the Olympics was scheduled to be decided at the IOC General Assembly in Lima on September 13. The 2024 Olympic Games were agreed to be held in Paris to commemorate the centenary, and Los Angeles took the rights to host the next Games in 2028.
